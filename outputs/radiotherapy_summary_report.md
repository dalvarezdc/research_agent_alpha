### What is Radiotherapy?

Radiotherapy (radiation therapy) uses high-energy radiation beams to damage cancer cell DNA, preventing their growth and division. It can be delivered externally via linear accelerators or internally through brachytherapy. Treatment is typically fractionated over several weeks to maximize cancer cell kill while allowing normal tissue recovery. Modern techniques like IMRT and IGRT improve precision and reduce side effects.
